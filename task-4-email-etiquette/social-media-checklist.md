# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for Students
Here are the points for students to do and not to do while being on social media...

## Do's
- Maintain a professional profile
- Think before posting online
- Use strong privacy settings
- Share useful and positive content
- Respect others in online discussions

## Don'ts
- Do not share personal information publicly
- Avoid posting offensive or harmful content
- Do not spread misinformation
- Avoid spreading rumors and hate
- Do not click suspicious links
