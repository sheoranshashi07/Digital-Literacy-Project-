# Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your OTP, PIN, or banking details with anyone.
2. Do not accept unknown UPI payment requests.
3. Always verify payment links before clicking them.
4. Use strong and unique passwords for online accounts.
5. Enable two-factor authentication whenever possible.
6. Avoid using public WiFi for financial transactions.
7. Install apps only from official app stores.
8. Regularly check bank statements for suspicious activity.

## Reporting Cybercrime in India
If you become a victim of cybercrime, report it immediately:

National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
