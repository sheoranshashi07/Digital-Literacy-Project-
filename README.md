## Project Overview

This repository contains my Digital Literacy Project created as part of the course **CSE0001 – Digital Literacy**.  

The objective of this project is to develop essential digital skills required for students in the modern academic and professional environment. The project covers topics such as digital awareness, building a professional online presence, coding platforms, professional communication, and cybercrime awareness.

## Task Summaries:

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
Created a visual infographic explaining the concept of digital literacy, useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, and the importance of maintaining a professional online presence.

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
Set up professional profiles on platforms such as **GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle** to build a digital portfolio that can support future academic and career opportunities.

### Task 3 – Coding and Collaboration Platforms
Explored coding practice platforms by completing a beginner challenge and created a **Google Form quiz** to promote digital literacy awareness among students.

Google Form Quiz Link: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdr-hGvOpus1OlcbWGtfo4UVAtnUpWKQQevxVUeCFCQxnWOig/viewform?usp=publish-editor

### Task 4 – Email Etiquette and Professional Communication
Drafted professional emails demonstrating correct email structure and tone. Also created a **social media do's and don'ts checklist** to encourage responsible online behavior.

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Case Study
Researched a cybercrime scenario related to **UPI payment fraud** and created a case study along with a prevention checklist to educate students about staying safe online.

## Key Learning Outcomes

Through this project, I learned:

- The importance of digital literacy in education and professional life  
- How to build a professional online presence  
- The role of coding platforms in skill development  
- Proper digital communication and email etiquette  
- Awareness of cybercrime and methods to prevent online fraud
